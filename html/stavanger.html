WHAT TO SEE

1. STAVANGER

This discreet town, located in the Norway of the fjords, has some 8,000 wooden houses of different styles. Few things surpass a relaxing walk through the narrow streets of the center of the city. And on sunny days, you can stop for a coffee in one of the terraces of the Øvre Holmegate street, where you will find several houses painted with bright colors.

2. GLOPPEDALEN

Gloppedalen is narrow valley in Rogaland. Most famous for Gloppura (Gloppedalsura), one of the country's largest rock tours, which for a mile long fills the entire valley between the vertical mountain walls. Gloppedalsura is located on the water slide. Between Gloppedalsvatnet, which has a drain to Dirdalsåna in the northeast and Bjerkreimsvassdraget in the southwest.
