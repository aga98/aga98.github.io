WHAT TO SEE

1. CATEDRALE DI SANTA MARIA DEL FIORE

The Arco Naturale is a natural arch on the east coast of the island of Capri. Dating from the Paleolithic age, it is the remains of a collapsed grotto.The arch spans 12 m at a height of 18 m above ground and consists of limestone

2. PALAZZO PITTI

The funicular runs between the port in Marina Grande and the Piazzetta in Capri town above, taking just a few minutes to complete the trip. The Capri stop is behind the Piazzetta. Courses run every 15 minutes (for example: 9:00, 9:15, 9:30...), though during the most crowded times the funicular runs non-stop. The funicular climb is a beautiful experience. It allows you to appreciate the landscape: sea, vegetacón and constructions that are a mime for the view. Once up you find yourself with commercial stores of the best brands, in clothes, accessories, etc. in addition to gourmet places.
